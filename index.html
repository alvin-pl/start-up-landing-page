Our Mission


We want to create the most safe and clean environment wherever YOU go. This pandemic has showed that germs are everywhere and you can minimize you exposure by carrying Hanitize with you or keeping it nearby.

Hanitize mini

The mini allows you to take it anywhere. Put it in your pocket, take it on the go. It can fit in your shorts, pants, shirt pocket, or even on a key chain.

Hanitizer 2000

Hanitizer stationary

This one here is stationary. Keep it by your desk, your counter, your bed, or couch. It will always be closeby for your use. Don't take it with you, just place it at different stations around your way.

Hanitizer 3000

Hanitizer Deluxe

It is a multipack so you never run out. Share with family members, share with friends. Make sure everyone is safe and clean. You don't to be the person spreading germs.
